# Insta-RealEstate Brandbook v1.0

## 1. Core Identity
**Mission**: Automating high-reach property marketing for elite real estate agencies.
**Value Proposition**: "The 30-Second Designer." Turning URL slop into "Portrait Impact" carousels.
**Audience**: High-net-worth real estate professionals, boutique agencies in Cascais, Lisbon, Porto, and global prime markets.

---

## 2. Visual Palette (The "Prime" Spectrum)
*   **Obsidian Black** (`#0a0a0a`): The primary background. Signifies depth, exclusivity, and focus.
*   **Luxury Gold** (`#d4af37`): Accent color for titles, buttons, and "Pro" status. Signifies wealth, success, and quality.
*   **Platinum Gray** (`#a0a0a0`): Secondary text color. Low-contrast and sophisticated.
*   **Glassmorphism Glass** (`rgba(255, 255, 255, 0.02)`): Card backgrounds with `backdrop-filter: blur(20px)`.

---

## 3. Typography
*   **Headlines**: **Playfair Display** (Serif). Used for all `h1`, `h2`, `h3` and the logo text. Conveys heritage and boutique authority.
*   **Body Copy**: **Inter** (Sans-Serif). Used for readability, feature lists, and navigation. Conveys modern efficiency.
*   **Scaling**: All headers must use `clamp()` for fluid responsive behavior.

---

## 4. Logo Guidelines
*   **The "IR" Monogram**: A golden monogram emblem on an obsidian square.
*   **Usage**: Must always be used in the top-left navigation or centralized for marketing assets.
*   **Margin**: Minimum 1rem clear space around the emblem.

---

## 5. UI/UX Rules (Zero-Slop Standard)
*   **Mobile Grid**: Single-column vertical stack ONLY. No overlapping elements.
*   **Buttons**: Pill-shaped, golden gradient (`linear-gradient(135deg, #d4af37, #b3891d)`), uppercase typography with `letter-spacing: 0.05rem`.
*   **Lists**: No default browser bullets. Use the custom `Zap` or `CheckCircle` icons.
*   **Navigation**: Non-fixed on mobile to prevent content overlap.
*   **Spacing**: High-end agencies demand breathing room. Minimum `padding: 4rem` between sections on mobile; `8rem` on desktop.

---

## 6. Tone of Voice
*   **Authoritative**: We don't "help" agents; we "provide the standard."
*   **Efficient**: Time-saving is the primary KPI.
*   **Premium**: Avoid conversational "slop." Use precise, direct language.
